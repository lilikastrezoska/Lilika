Every one of us has that little something which makes our lives complete, something that defines us on many levels and makes us the people that we are. While the human kind revels in habit, we are also creatures of passion which is the drive that helps us pursue our dreams. My passion is represented through a small and simple device with endless possibilities - my camera. I am mesmerized by the notion of a moment. I find it enthralling to be able to capture it, regardless of the fact if it is just a simple glimmer of hope in someones eyes or a complete immortalization of a landscape. I am Lilika Strezoska, wedding photo-maker and truth-teller; situated in Berlin and accessible around the world. If my photographs resound or associate with you in any way- if they influence you to feel things, don't mind to send me a note and I would love to chat more about photographing and colaborating with you.
